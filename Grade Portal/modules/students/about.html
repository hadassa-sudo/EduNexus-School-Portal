About the Portal

The STI Student Portal keeps your necessary student information in this free and easy-to-use platform. With the STI Student Portal, you can now:

  • View your grades with just a quick tap of a button
  • Know your day-to-day class schedule complete with classroom details and professors
  • Get the latest STI news from all over the STI network
  • Access important academic resources and information
